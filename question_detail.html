How do I deploy my Laravel app to Heroku?

Posted by John Doe on May 25, 2025 in Web Development

Tags: laravel heroku

Answers

Make sure your database configuration uses environment variables supported by Heroku. Also, use a buildpack for PHP and clear caches after deployment.

Answered by Jane Smith on May 26, 2025

Add `php artisan config:cache` and `php artisan migrate` in your release phase. Ensure your `APP_KEY` is set in Heroku config vars too.
